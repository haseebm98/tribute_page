Dr. Nikola Tesla

The genius who discovered free energy

Here is the time line of Tesla's life:

  • 1859 - Nikola Tesla was born, in Smiljan, Serbia
  • 1870 - Tesla contracts cholera, has a near death experience
  • 1875 - Tesla enrolls in college
  • 1880 - Tesla moves to Prague to study philosophy
  • 1881 - Tesla begins his career as an engineer
  • 1885 - Tesla submits some of his first patents
  • 1890 - Tesla begins his work on wireless power transmission
  • 1891 - Tesla patents the Tesla Coil
  • 1943 - Nikola Tesla dies in a hotel in New York
